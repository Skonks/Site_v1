Yukie Mayuzumi

Lore

Yukie Mayuzumi(黛 由紀江) is one of the main heroines in Maji De Watashi Ni Koi Shinasai! anime and manga. She is a newcomer to the Kazama Family who has the ambition to make one hundred friends, the daughter of Taisei Mayuzumi and one of the love interests to Yamato Naoe in Majikoi! and Majikoi! S. Yukie is the recent and latest addition to the Four Devas, a group made of the strongest fighters in the world. This is a fact that is relatively unknown to the public however, due to Yukie's withdrawn personality. Yukie is a fighter of most special interest to Momoyo Kawakami due to her strength and potential with Momoyo watching for her development with a desire to face Yukie in a decent fight. Yukie represents Respect (礼, Rei), one of the seven virtues of martial arts.

Appearance

Yukie is a fairly tall and soft pale-skinned girl with thigh-length deep forest-green hair that is tied in two low pigtails by two white ribbons with square bangs, dark blue eyes, a voluptuous figure, she keeps herself well kept that gives off a yamato nadeshiko aura.
